# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Weave is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussions and reviews.
- Focus critique on code and ideas, not people.
- Share context and evidence when disagreeing.
- Help keep issues/PRs actionable and on-topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainer: `https://github.com/rotsl`.
